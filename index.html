Our Menu

Welcome to Fantasy Fudge, the sweetest little secret of Aparna 1

Nestled right in the heart of our cozy community, we’re not your everyday bakery—we’re a warm and delicious extension of home. From oven-fresh cookies and melt-in-your-mouth pastries to cheesy pizzas and comforting brews, everything we make is sprinkled with love, laughter, and a whole lot of flavor.

Fantasy Fudge is exclusively for our Aparna 1 family. That’s right! No outsiders, no long queues, no city crowd—just neighbors, friends, and familiar faces enjoying delightful treats in their own little sweet spot. Whether you’re grabbing a sandwich on a lazy afternoon, celebrating a birthday with our signature cakes, or just dropping by for a chat over cappuccino and cookies, this is your bakery. Made by the community, for the community—with a whole lot of fudge-tastic magic.

So come on over, let’s make every bite a memory

For the Cheese Lovers

Three ingredients. Infinite Love.

Simple, classic, and utterly delicious—our Margherita Pizza is a timeless favorite that lets fresh ingredients shine. Made with a hand-tossed crust, it’s topped with a rich, tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and a sprinkle of aromatic basil. Baked to golden perfection, this pizza is the perfect balance of crisp, creamy, and herby goodness. Sometimes, less really is more!

Mixing traditional flavours with pizza

Bringing the bold flavors of India to a classic favorite, the Tandoori Chicken Pizza features succulent chunks of tandoori-marinated chicken, red onions, and bell peppers on a base of spicy tandoori sauce and melted cheese.

This fusion pizza delivers smoky, spicy, and savory notes that make it a must-try for lovers of desi-style indulgence.

For the Paneer warriors

A fusion twist to your sandwich craving, our Paneer Tikka Sandwich is filled with tender cubes of marinated paneer grilled to perfection, along with fresh veggies and a creamy spiced mayo

Served between toasted bread slices, it’s the ideal balance of smoky tikka flavor and satisfying crunch—perfect for a filling, feel-good meal.

Sandwich with Indian twist

Packed with protein and bursting with flavor, the Meat Lover Chicken Sandwich is a hearty treat for non-veg fans.

Stuffed with juicy grilled chicken, spicy chicken salami, and a layer of shredded chicken, all complemented by melted cheese and zesty sauces. Toasted golden brown and loaded with meat in every bite, this sandwich is a full-on feast.

Healthy & Tasty Salad

A timeless classic, our Caesar Salad combines crisp romaine lettuce tossed in a creamy, tangy Caesar dressing made with hints of garlic, lemon, and parmesan.

Topped with crunchy golden croutons and a generous sprinkle of grated parmesan cheese, it offers a refreshing bite with a rich umami depth. Light yet satisfying, it’s perfect as a starter or a wholesome main when paired with grilled chicken or paneer (optional add-ons).
